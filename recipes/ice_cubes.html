ice cube recipe

A delicious meal you can make with minimal time commitment!

Great for college students in a time crunch!

ingredients

  • 2 cups of water
  • 2 tablespoons of water (optional)

steps

  1. empty ice cubes that are in tray
  2. take tray to sink and fill with water
  3. place tray in freezer
  4. wait until frozen
